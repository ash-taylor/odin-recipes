Spicy Spinach & Tiger Prawn Gnocchi

Description

A fresh take on traditional gnocchi. Sustainably sourced tiger prawns meet pillows of potato gnocchi in a spicy tomato broth.
Baby spinach for greens and a pinch of chilli flakes for kick. Tangy, spicy and herby - this dish hits the whole palate.
And it's ready in 20 minutes or less.

Ingredients

  • Â½ tsp red chilli flakes
  • 2 tsp cornflour
  • 3 garlic clove
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 shallot
  • 160g baby spinach
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (Celery)
  • 110g baby plum tomatoes
  • 200g raw peeled black tiger prawns (Crustaceans, Fish)
  • 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 250g potato gnocchi

Steps

  1. Boil a kettle. Dissolve the cornflour in a jug with 50ml cold water.
    Crumble in the stock cube and top up with 200ml boiling water.
  2. Finely dice the shallot. Finely chop or crush the garlic.
    Heat a medium frying pan with 11/2 tbsp oil on medium heat.
    Add the shallot, whole tomatoes and garlic and cook for 5-7 mins, stirring occasionally,
    until softened. Season with sea salt.
  3. Add the oregano, chilli flakes (to taste) and vinegar. Cook for 30 seconds, then pour in the stock.
    Add the prawns and simmer for 6-8 mins, until the prawns are cooked and the broth has thickened slightly.
    Season with sea salt and black pepper.
  4. Meanwhile, half-fill a large saucepan with salted boiling water on high heat. Add the gnocchi and simmer for 2-3 mins,
    or until they float to the surface, then drain. Add the cooked gnocchi and spinach to the broth and cook for 1-2 mins,
    until the spinach has wilted.
  5. Serve in bowls and garnish with a pinch of chilli flakes, if desired.
